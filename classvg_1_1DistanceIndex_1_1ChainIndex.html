int64_t vg::DistanceIndex::ChainIndex::chainDistance ( pair< id_t, bool >  start,
pair< id_t, bool >  end 
)

Distance between two node sides in a chain. id_t values specify the nodes, and bool values specify the sides. Side orientations are relative to the node's orientation in the chain, so if reading through the chain in its forward orientation you encounter the node in reverse, then true is the left side of the node and false is the right side.
